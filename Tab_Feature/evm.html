Why INDIA is not ready for electric vehicles ?

  • The Customer Price is prohibitive
  • High price point is the biggest entry barrier
  • Range anxiety kills the spirit and potential consumer
  • Lack of Charging infra and battery charging time is high
  • Not all can install chargers especially those living beyond first floor residences
  • Super slow domestic change kills the enthusiasm
  • Absent public charging infrastructure belies all belief
  • Younger millennial lot and the senior citizens will avoid such purchases
  • Electric 2W tenants could face landlord backlash for changing from IC Engine to Electric Vehicle
  • Open parking lots leads to safety concerns of thefts
  • Low wattage defeats the purpose of clean energy vehicles
  • Auto companies are oblivion of consumer needs and on ground problems
  • Dealers too avoid investing in expensive fast charging infrastructure
  • Too complicated technology for Indian market and consumer needs
  • Limited range of around 250-300 kms with aircon prevent intercity mobility
